        Guide to using this application

        Getting started

        Quickly filter through the International Real Estate Guide to find the information you require by selecting a Country and or Category and Sub Category from the Filter List.

        As you begin choosing Your Results section below the Filter Lists will begin to populate with your filtered information.

        If no Countries or multiple Countries are selected, then the Results section displays the filtered information for each country within a collapsed panel. These can be opened and closed at will by the user.

        Once you have found the information you require a number of options are available to choose from, these include.

        • Send to a Colleague
        • Get In Touch
        • Download PDF

        You are able to reset your selected filter options by pressing the Reset button at the top of each of the Filter Lists.
